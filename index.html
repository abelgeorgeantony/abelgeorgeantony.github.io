Hello!
My name is Abel George Antony.
I am old.
I come from the lowest altitude in India.
Hobby: Staring at this website until I question what I am doing with my life.

This is not a portfolio, you won't find a resume or trendy buzzwords here. This is just my personal corner in the internet. Part of the inspiration for a site like this is the old internet, where people had their own weird, hand-coded websites for no particular reason other than to just exist online. So, I built one(one of the many I've made over the years; most of the old ones have been lost in the digital void). There is no objective here, no target audience, and nobody is trying to impress you about anything. My interests are kind of all over the place. If you made it here, feel free to look around. It's mostly things you probably won't find interesting. If it does interest you, reach out to me! We could chat.
